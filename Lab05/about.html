What is Southern Tickets Online?

Southern Tickets Online is an event-ticketing service. This non-profit program provides comprehensive ticketing services for a wide variety of University event presenters and venues.
Currently, Southern Tickets Online provides ticketing services for:
  • Shryock Auditorium
  • McLeod Theater
  • Christian H. Moe Theater
  • SIU School of Music
  • SIU Department of Theater
  • McLeod Summer Playhouse
  • Southern Illinois Symphony Orchestra
